# Mobile-App-Development-Project

A fun and interactive app to test how well your friends know you! 🎉

## Features

- 📋 **Create Custom Quizzes**: Personalize quizzes with questions tailored to your personality or shared experiences.
- 🔗 **Generate and Share Links**: Create a unique link to share your quiz with friends easily.
- 🧩 **Match Answers and Earn Points**: Friends earn points by matching your answers, making it a fun and competitive experience.
